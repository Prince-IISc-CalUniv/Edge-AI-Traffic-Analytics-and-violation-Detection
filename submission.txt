PROJECT TITLE:
    Edge AI Traffic Analytics and violation Detection

PROJECT DESCRIPTION:
    A real-time, multi-task traffic monitoring system deployed  that simultaneously detects helmet violations, triple-riding,
    overspeeding, traffic congestion, and road potholes using three compressed
    YOLOv8/v11 models (NCNN FP16). The system processes live camera feeds
    entirely on-device with no cloud dependency.
